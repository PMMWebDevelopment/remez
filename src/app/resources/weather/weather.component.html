Weather in Hebrew

Here are some phrases which can come in handy for talking about the weather in Hebrew.

?מה מזג האביר היום
Mah mzeg ha-aveer ha-yom?
How's the weather today?
?מה יהיה מזג האויר מחר
Mah yiheyeh mzeg ha-avir ha-yom?
How will the weather be tomorrow?
